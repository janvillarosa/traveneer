Hi! I'm Eileen

The Manual is a design journal for the web that examines why we design the way we do. Design, by nature, is a practice of connecting people; the web, in its purest sense, is a mechanism that does the same. We evaluate both.

We’ve published three superb issues exclusively in print format, with our fourth issue coming in October. But now we’re moving beyond print to meet you on every platform –– and we’re experimenting with that path by releasing an open web edition, ebooks, audiobooks, and more.

What’s changing?

1. We’re publishing a free and open web edition.
Previously, The Manual has existed only in copyrighted, printed books. Going forward, we’re allowing anyone to access and share all our issues online under a Creative Commons license. This will help us all contribute to the conversation around designing for the web in a more meaningful way.

2. We’re publishing everywhere.
In addition to our print edition, you can now find us on any ebook device and in audiobook form. You tell us where you want to enjoy The Manual, and we’ll meet you there.

3. We’re offering “pay if you want” subscriptions.
We’re making The Manual openly available through our web edition, supported by pay-if-you-want subscriptions. All our issues will be available online in a beautiful, responsive site, and you can pledge for a recurring subscription for the print, ebook, audiobook, and/or web editions to help support it.
